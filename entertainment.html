Ramojifilm City

Ramoji Film City is an integrated film studio facility located in Hyderabad, India. Spread over 1,666 acres (674 ha), it is the largest film studio complex in the world and as such has been certified by the Guinness World Records. It was establised by Telugu media tycoon Ramoji Rao in 1996.To begin with, Ramoji Film City tour offers behind the scenes experiences of live movie sets and shoots-in-progress. Plus, there are restaurants, a dedicated area for shopping, rides etc. So much so that the Guinness Book of World Records has listed it in its reputed log for covering an area of 2,500 acres.The best time to visit Ramoji Film City is during winter as Hyderabad gets very hot during summers. You should visit Ramoji Film City from October to February, when the weather is cool and at its best.Carrying arms, explosives, inflammable materials, food articles, alcoholic beverages and narcotic substances are not permitted in RAMOJI FILM CITY. Animals and Pets are not allowed in RAMOJI FILM CITY.
